About Us

Perched on the slopes of Mt. Bachelor, Alpine Provisions is the premier stop for high-quality nutrition tailored for the adventurous. Whether you're skiing down powdery slopes, trekking on rugged trails, or anything in between, our food is designed to fuel your journey.

From high-protein breakfasts to nutrient-packed lunches and recovery essentials like electrolyte drinks and herbal teas, we ensure you stay energized and ready for your next challenge. Locally sourced ingredients, expert-crafted meals, and a love for the outdoors define our passion for providing nourishment that matches your ambitions.
